about the reception

venue

The party will take place at Matilda-Talo, a historic “people’s house” on the shore of lake Matilda. The venue includes an outdoor dance floor and lakeside sauna.

attire

Formal wear, colourful, summery. Consider the Finnish weather in August. Bring a swimsuit if you would like a late evening dip from the sauna!

Staying at
Mathildedal

Our wedding will take place in Mathildedal, around 30 minutes from Salo. There are many accommodation options in the village from hotel rooms to cabins for larger groups.

We have a discount code (Group15) for those wishing to book a cabin at Matilda Villas for the whole weekend with a larger group (4–8 people).

More information: visitmathildedal.fi
Matilda Villas: www.matildavillas.fi
